How to read it

Four simple rules

1. Lower number = sooner
If you are #1, you get the next flex opening. If you are #2, you are next after that.
2. Fewer games stays in front
On normal weeks, players with fewer season appearances stay closer to the front of the line.
3. Ties use season totals
If two players have played the same number of games, the app breaks the tie with season points, then goals, goal keeps, and clean sheets.
4. Final night uses points first
On the final matchday, the order flips and season performance takes priority for the finale.

Only players who are both flex and eligible appear in this line. Learn more about tiers and queue rules.
