How it works

Simple rules

Fewer games = front of line
If you've played 2 games and someone else played 4, you go first.
Ties break on season totals
Same number of games? Higher points go first, then goals, goal keeps, and clean sheets.
Final matchday is different
On the last night of the season, the line flips fully to season totals: highest points still go first.
